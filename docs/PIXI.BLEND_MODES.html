@pixi/constants

PIXI.BLEND_MODES

BLEND_MODES

Various blend modes supported by PIXI.

IMPORTANT - The WebGL renderer only supports the NORMAL, ADD, MULTIPLY and SCREEN blend modes. Anything else will silently act like NORMAL.
