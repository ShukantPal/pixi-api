HalfEdge

class HalfEdge
implements ILinkedListNode

A directed edge from its origin to destination.

{@code HalfEdge} is a node of the following circular linked-lists:

  • the list of edges in the whole mesh: HalfEdge#next
  • the list of edges around its left face: HalfEdge#lnext
  • the list of edges directed from its origin vertex: HalfEdge#onext
  • the list of edges directed to its destination vertex: HalfEdge#dnext
  • the list of edges around its right face: HalfEdge#rnext
